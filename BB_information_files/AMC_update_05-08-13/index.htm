Affiliated Mortgage Company (AMC) is a wholly owned subsidiary of Benchmark Bank of Plano, Texas and is currently comprised of correspondent and retail lending divisions.

Affiliated Mortgage Company’s retail division offers a variety of home loan products tailored to meet your purchase and refinancing needs. We look forward to showing you how industry experience and knowledge coupled with superior customer service can make the loan process seamless.

Affiliated Mortgage Company’s correspondent division purchases agency quality whole loans - servicing released - from a nationwide network of mortgage lenders. Our customers consist of banks, savings banks, mortgage banking corporations and credit unions.
